Still Speaking

A structured, research-based curriculum for complete beginners — designed to honour and support Indigenous language revitalization across the Northern Plains.

⚗ Experimental Project Notice: Still Speaking is an early-stage website exploring the use of AI development tools to produce widely distributable and accessible language learning material for underserved Indigenous language communities. Content is grounded in peer-reviewed and community-published sources. This platform is in active development and does not replace instruction by fluent speakers, elders, or certified language educators. Community feedback and corrections are actively welcomed.
