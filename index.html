Still Speaking

A structured, research-based curriculum for complete beginners — designed to honour and support Indigenous language revitalization across the Northern Plains and beyond.

⚗ Experimental Project Notice: Still Speaking is an early-stage, experimental website exploring the use of AI development tools to produce widely distributable and accessible language learning material for underserved Indigenous language communities. Content accuracy is grounded in peer-reviewed and community-published sources; however, this platform is in active development and should not replace instruction by fluent speakers, elders, or certified language educators. We actively welcome corrections, contributions, and community feedback.
